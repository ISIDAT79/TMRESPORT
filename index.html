Since 2022, TMR Esports has ignited India’s competitive gaming scene with electrifying events, epic tournaments, and a passionate gaming community. We bring together the best teams and brands to create unforgettable esports moments.

To elevate players, teams, and fans through innovative tournaments, fair play, and a vibrant gaming culture.
